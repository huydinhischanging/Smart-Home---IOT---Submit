Healthcare IoT: Heart Rate & HRV Analysis Pipeline

flowchart TD
    %% =========================
    %% 1) Hardware Layer
    %% =========================
    subgraph Data_Acquisition [Data Acquisition Layer]
        A["Coospo H6 chest strap"] -->|BLE Advert.| B["GATT HR Service (UUID 0x180D)"]
        B -->|Notify| C["Python BLE Bridge (Bleak)"]
        C --> D["Parse HR Measurement (UUID 0x2A37)"]
        D --> E["Extract BPM + Timestamp"]
    end

    E -->|MQTT Publish| F["Topic: home/sensors/heart_rate"]

    %% =========================
    %% 2) Backend Intake
    %% =========================
    subgraph Backend_Ingestion [Backend Ingestion Layer]
        F --> G["Backend MQTT Listener"]
        G --> H["Heart-rate Handler"]
        H --> I[("Persist HR Record (PostgreSQL)")]
        H --> J["Push Socket.IO: sensor_update"]
    end

    %% =========================
    %% 3) Signal Processing & HRV Pipeline
    %% =========================
    subgraph HRV_Engine [HRV Feature Engineering]
        H --> K["Sliding Window (BPM Samples)"]
        K --> L["BPM to RR Interval: 
RR(ms) = 60000 / BPM"]

        L --> RMSSD["RMSSD Calculation"]
        L --> SDNN["SDNN Calculation"]
        L --> PNN50["pNN50 Calculation"]

        RMSSD --> M["HRV Feature Vector"]
        SDNN --> M
        PNN50 --> M
    end

    %% =========================
    %% 4) Intelligence & Alerts
    %% =========================
    subgraph Risk_Assessment [Intelligence Layer]
        M --> N["Anomaly Engine (Rules + ML)"]
        N --> O{"Risk Level?"}
        
        O -->|Normal| P["Soft Update UI"]
        O -->|Elevated| Q["Warning UI"]
        O -->|High| R["Notify Caregiver (SMS/Push)"]
        O -->|Critical| S["Immediate Emergency Alert"]
    end

    P & Q & R & S --> T["Web + Mobile Dashboard"]

* Chú thích: RMSSD, SDNN, và pNN50 là các chỉ số quan trọng để đánh giá mức độ căng thẳng (Stress) và sức khỏe tim mạch.
